Program Structure
3 phases, each building on the last
Phase 1

Foundation

Learn barbell movements from scratch. Light load, form first. Band and dumbbell work alongside to build stability and muscle endurance.

Weeks 1–4
Phase 2

Build

Progressive barbell overload every 1–2 weeks. Volume increases. Cardio extends to 25 min. Pulley isolation work added to upper days.

Weeks 5–8
Phase 3

Intensity

Supersets on strength days. Full-body barbell circuit Fridays. 30-min cardio. This is where the recomp really accelerates.

Weeks 9–12

Nutrition
Fuel the recomp

Protein Priority

  • Target ~130–145g protein daily (≈0.6g/lb)
  • Chicken, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, canned fish, lean beef
  • Protein at every meal protects muscle while burning fat

Calories

  • Modest deficit: 300–400 cal/day below maintenance
  • Don't skip meals on strength days — fuel matters
  • Carbs around workouts help performance and recovery

Hydration

  • 80–100+ oz of water daily
  • Pinch of salt or electrolytes on heavy training days
  • Thirst is often mistaken for hunger — drink first
The scale may not move much — that's normal. Recomp shows in how clothes fit, how strong she feels, and how the barbell numbers move. Track all three, not just weight.

Program Structure
3 phases, each building on the last
Phase 1

Foundation

Master the movements. Build joint stability, motor patterns, and baseline strength. Lower load, higher focus on form.

Weeks 1–4
Phase 2

Build

Add load and volume. Introduce more challenging variations. Cardio duration increases. Endurance and strength develop together.

Weeks 5–8
Phase 3

Intensity

Push the pace. Supersets, heavier loads, and full-body metabolic circuits. This is where the body really changes.

Nutrition
Fuel the recomp

Protein Priority

  • Target ~130–145g protein daily
  • Chicken, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, canned fish, lean beef
  • Protein at every meal protects muscle while burning fat

Calories

  • Modest deficit: 300–400 cal/day below maintenance
  • Don't skip meals on strength days — you need fuel to recover
  • Carbs around workouts support performance and recovery

Hydration

  • Aim for 80–100+ oz of water daily
  • Add a pinch of salt or electrolytes on heavy sweat days
  • Thirst is often mistaken for hunger — drink first
The scale may not move much. That's normal — and it doesn't mean the plan isn't working. Recomp shows up in how clothes fit, how strong you feel, and how much energy you have. Track those, not just the number.
